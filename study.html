Our Study

1. Introduce 90 participants to a new sport

2. The ninety participants will be divided into three groups of 30

3. The first group will use our app to learn a sport, the second will be taught by a coach, and the third will try and teach themselves

4. Each group will work 3 hours a day for 1 week on learning a new sport

5. After 1 week, the progress between the three groups will be compared

6. Each group will complete a survey asking about how easy it was to learn a new sport through their respective method
