The Problem

There are plenty of sports apps out there for tracking how many calories you've burned or how many steps you've taken.

But currently there isn't a place to share all that hard work you've put in ad compare it to your friends. If you wanted to see how you'd stack up against your favorite atheletes you'd have to indidually look up all their stats, calculate yours, and compare them.

We believe the best way to learn is by doing, and with access to tips, videos, and ststs from top atheletes, we belive you can follow along and all learn and improve together

Recap

There are a few main points we hope to fix.

High Scores

Keeping Stats All in one place

We hope to use are app as a repository of records and stats for professional athelets and aspiring athletes alike

challenging Yourself

Keeping up with your friends

Our studies report that people prefer to work out together but not everyone has time to meet up at the gym, thus our app hopes to foster an ecosystem for feiends and professionals alike to push eachother to the bect level

Learning From The Best

Knowedge for everyone

Not everyone has the money to hire a personal trainer and of those who do, very few have the money and contacts to learn from top athletes, our app brings thier knowledge right to the user and provides motivation to learn and keek improving.
