Development

Our development took place over a vew steps

Research

Scoping the playing field

We looked at popular fitness apps and looked at what we liked and what we didn't

Brainstorming

Getting ideas out there

As a group we all had different ideas on how to make our app so we threw out as many ideas as we could to see what stuck

Prototyping and Testing

Perfection comes from practice

We created some paper prototypes and tested using them and then modified our prototypes until we decided on a system that was easy to use and looked good to all of us

Future Plans:

We hope to partner wih gyms to integrate our app with state of the art spatial tracking to track the users movement in the court or on the field.

This would allow users to accurately measure their performance in games well as drills

This would also allow useres to follow along with the lessons in 3d and measure their movements so that the don't make bad habits. That way they learn it right the first time.
